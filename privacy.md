# Privacy policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

TourCycle does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external 
servers.

## Health data

TourCycle uses Apple HealthKit to read and write workout and heart rate data. 
This data is used solely to display your workout history within the app and 
is never shared with TourCycle's developer or any third party.

## Location

TourCycle does not access or collect your location.

## Data storage

All app data, including workout history, is stored locally on your device.
